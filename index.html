Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor of Accounting at Brigham Young University. My research examines topics in financial reporting, tax, and disclosure, with a focus on how economic incentives shape managerial behavior and regulatory enforcement. I have published in leading journals including The Accounting Review and Review of Accounting Studies.
